About Me

Hello! My name is Faza Fauzan, a freelance translator and programmer . With a background in Linguistics, I have a strong passion for technology, especially in AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP). I previously worked as a programmer at PT IMAMATEK for 9 months, focusing on business process automation and web development using JavaScript and the Biz-A framework. Now, I work as a freelance translator while continuing to explore new advancements in programming and artificial intelligence. As an introvert, I enjoy deep-diving into coding and expanding my knowledge in computational linguistics.
